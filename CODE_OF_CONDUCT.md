# Code of conduct

We want RealDone to be a focused, welcoming engineering community. Be respectful, assume good intent, critique ideas with evidence, and do not harass, demean, or expose private information about others.

Maintainers may edit or remove abusive, unsafe, spammy, or off-topic contributions and may restrict participation when necessary. Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers through the repository's security/contact channels.
